Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. This page explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights to request deletion.

What data we collect

When you use JobMatch, we may collect and store information such as:

  • Your account details (email and basic profile information).
  • Your CV / uploaded documents and extracted text.
  • Your job preferences, work experience, education, and skills.
  • Job applications and AI-generated content like cover letters.

How we use your data

The data we collect is used only to:

  • Generate and improve AI-based cover letters for your applications.
  • Match you with relevant job listings and personalize recommendations.

We do not sell your personal data or use it for unrelated advertising. Any processing is strictly to help you design better applications and find better job matches.

Data retention and deletion

You remain in control of your data. You can delete many items (such as documents and profile details) directly in the app. If you would like your account and associated data to be fully removed from our systems, you can request deletion at any time.

To request deletion, please contact our support team using the email associated with your account. Once we verify your identity, we will delete your data within a reasonable timeframe, except where we are legally required to retain certain records (for example, payment records).

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to our support team via the contact details provided in the app or on our landing page.
